## 1. Introduction

Welcome to AyahFind ("we," "our," or "the App"). AyahFind is a mobile application that identifies Quran recitations from audio recordings and provides translations, continuous playback, and Ramadan journey tracking. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Audio Data

When you use AyahFind's verse identification feature:
- We access your device's microphone to capture audio of Quran recitations
- Audio recordings are processed to identify verses using AI speech recognition (OpenAI Whisper) and audio fingerprinting (ACRCloud)
- **Audio recordings are NOT stored on our servers** - they are processed ephemerally and immediately deleted after identification
- Audio is transmitted securely via HTTPS to third-party processing services
- Only the identification results (verse number, surah name) are returned to your device

### 2.2 Reading Progress Data

We store your Ramadan Journey reading progress:
- Completed reading sessions
- Journey goals and milestones
- Surah reading history
- **This data is stored locally on your device only** using secure device storage
- We do not transmit or store this data on external servers
- You can clear this data by uninstalling the app

### 2.3 Usage Data

We do **NOT** collect analytics, crash reports, or usage statistics. The app does not include any tracking or analytics services.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Identify Quran verses from audio recordings
- Provide verse translations and tafsir
- Track your reading progress locally
- Enable continuous audio playback
- Deliver the core features of the app

## 4. Data Storage & Security

We implement appropriate security measures:
- All network communications use **HTTPS encryption**
- Audio is processed in-memory and not saved to device storage
- Local progress data is stored securely on your device
- No data is stored on our servers
- We do not maintain user accounts or databases

## 5. Third-Party Services

AyahFind uses the following third-party services:

**Audio Processing:**
- **OpenAI Whisper API** - Audio transcription for verse identification - [Privacy Policy](https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy)
- **ACRCloud** - Audio fingerprinting for verse identification - [Privacy Policy](https://www.acrcloud.com/privacy)

**Content Delivery:**
- **Quran.com API** - Quran text and translations
- **EveryAyah.com** - Quran audio recitations (Mishary Alafasy)

We do not control these third-party services. Please review their privacy policies to understand how they handle data.

## 6. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access:** Request information about data we hold (none, as we don't store personal data)
- **Deletion:** Clear local progress data by uninstalling the app
- **Opt-out:** Disable microphone access through device settings (will disable identification feature)
- **Revoke Permissions:** Remove microphone permission at any time via device settings

## 7. Children's Privacy

AyahFind is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app's content is educational and religious in nature, appropriate for family use under parental guidance.

## 8. Microphone Permission

AyahFind requires microphone access **solely for the purpose of identifying Quran recitations**. You can revoke this permission at any time through your device settings, though this will disable the verse identification feature. The app will continue to work for reading Quran text and listening to recitations.

## 9. Data Sharing

We do **NOT** sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. The only data sharing that occurs is:
- Audio transmitted to OpenAI and ACRCloud for ephemeral processing (deleted immediately after identification)
- No personal information is shared

## 10. Permissions Required

### Android:
- **RECORD_AUDIO** - Capture audio for verse identification
- **INTERNET** - Fetch Quran content and process audio
- **POST_NOTIFICATIONS** - Display playback controls and journey reminders
- **WAKE_LOCK** - Keep audio playing in background
- **FOREGROUND_SERVICE** - Enable continuous audio playback

### iOS:
- **Microphone Access** - Record audio for verse identification
- **Background Audio** - Continue playback when app is in background

## 11. Information We Do NOT Collect

- Personal information (name, email, phone number, address)
- Location data
- Contacts, photos, or other personal files
- Analytics or usage tracking data
- Device identifiers for advertising
- Payment information (app is free)
